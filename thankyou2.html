The Professional Lifeguard Foundation is a non-profit organization, begun in 1996, which provides scholarship awards to Los Angeles County Lifeguards who are enrolled in full-time college or graduate course work.

Thank you for your donation. You are helping to make Professional Lifeguard scholarships a reality for Los Angeles County lifeguards.
