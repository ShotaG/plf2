The Professional Lifeguard Foundation is a non-profit organization, founded in 1996, which provides scholarship awards to Los Angeles County Lifeguards who are enrolled in full-time college or graduate course work.
The Professional Lifeguard Foundation needs your help to continue to support as many scholarships as possible. Donations are of enormous assistance to these dedicated young men and women.
